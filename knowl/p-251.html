We have finally arrived at the notion of a basis in a Hilbert space (indexed by \(\mathbb{N}\)). A complete orthonormal sequence in \(\hilbert\) is also called an orthonormal basis of \(\hilbert\text{.}\) The issue with identifying a basis is typically not orthogonality or norm, but instead completeness. After all, in infinite dimensions, there are lots of places to hide.
