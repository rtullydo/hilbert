Let \(e_1, \ldots, e_n\) be an orthonormal system in an inner product space \(\hilbert\) and let \(x \in \hilbert\text{.}\) The closest point \(y\) of \(\span\{e_1, \ldots, e_n\}\) to \(x\) is

\begin{equation*} y = \sum_{i=1}^n \ip{x}{e_i} e_i, \end{equation*}

and the distance \(d = \norm{x-y}\) from \(x\) to \(\span\{e_1, \ldots, e_n\}\) is given by

\begin{equation*} d^2 = \norm{x}^2 - \sum_{i=1}^n \abs{\ip{x}{e_i}}^2. \end{equation*}
