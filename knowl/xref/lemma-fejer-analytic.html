Lemma 6.2.3.

The function \(K_m\) satisfies the following properties:
  1. \begin{equation*} K_m(t) \geq 0, \hspace{1cm} \forall t \in \R, m \in \mathbb{Z}^{\geq 0}\text{;} \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} K_m(t)\, dt = 2\pi, \hspace{1cm} \forall m \in \mathbb{Z}^{\geq 0}; \end{equation*}
  3. For all \(\delta\) with \(0 \lt \delta \lt \pi\text{,}\)
    \begin{equation*} \int_{-\pi}^{-\delta} + \int_{\delta}^{\pi} K_m(t) \, dt \to 0 \text{ as } m \to \infty. \end{equation*}
