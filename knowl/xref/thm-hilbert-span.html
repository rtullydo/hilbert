Theorem 5.4.3.

Let \((e_n)_{n\in\mathbb{N}}\) be an orthonormal sequence in a Hilbert space \(\hilbert\text{.}\) The following are equivalent:
  1. \((e_n)\) is complete;
  2. \(\cl \spn \{e_n: n \in \mathbb{N}\} = \hilbert\text{;}\)
  3. \begin{equation*} \norm{x}^2 = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \abs{\ip{x}{e_n}}^2. \end{equation*}
