Theorem 5.4.2.

Let \((e_n)_{n\in\mathbb{N}}\) be a complete orthonormal sequence in a Hilbert space \(\hilbert\text{.}\) For any \(x \in \hilbert\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} x = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \ip{x}{e_n}e_n \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \norm{x}^2 = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \abs{\ip{x}{e_n}}^2. \end{equation*}
