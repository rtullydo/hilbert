Exercise 2.

\(\ell^1\) is the complex vector space of absolutely summable sequences of complex numbers - that is, all sequences \((x_n)\) such that

\begin{equation*} \sum_{n=1}^\infty \abs{x_n} \lt \infty, \end{equation*}

with entrywise addition and scalar multiplication. Show that the “1-norm”

\begin{equation*} \norm{x}_1 = \sum_{n = 1}^\infty \abs{x_n} \end{equation*}

meets the requirements of Definition 3.1.3.
