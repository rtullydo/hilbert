Let \((V, \norm{\cdot})\) be a normed space and let \(A \subseteq V\text{.}\) The linear span of \(A\), denoted \(\span A\text{,}\) is the intersection of all subspaces of \(V\) that contain \(A\text{.}\) The closed linear span of \(A\), denoted \(\cl \span A\text{,}\) is the intersection of all closed linear subspaces of \(V\) that contain \(A\text{.}\)
