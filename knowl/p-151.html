On the other hand, recall the space \(\ell_0\) Definition 3.2.2. In Example 3.2.1, we constructed a sequence of vectors in \(\ell_0\) that converged in \(\ell^2\) but not in \(\ell_0\text{.}\) That is, if we restricted our attention to the inner product space \(\ell_0\text{,}\) we could not perform any analysis using limits, because we might leave the space.
