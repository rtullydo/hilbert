Let \(e_1, \ldots, e_n\) be an orthonormal system in an inner product space \(\hilbert\text{,}\) let \(\la_1, \ldots, \la_n \in \C\) and suppose that \(x \in \hilbert\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} \norm{x - \sum_{i=1}^n \la_i e_i}^2 = \norm{x}^2 + \sum_{i=1}^n\abs{\la_i - c_i}^2 - \sum_{i=1}^n \abs{c_i}^2 \end{equation*}

where \(c_i = \ip{x}{e_i}\text{.}\)
