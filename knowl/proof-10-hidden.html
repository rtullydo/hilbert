By Theorem 3.2.3, \(\cl (\span A)\) is a subspace of \(V\text{.}\) It is closed and contains \(A\text{,}\) and so by Definition 3.2.4 it must be that \(\cl \span A \subseteq \cl(\span A)\text{.}\)

On the other hand, \(\cl \span A\) is closed and contains \(A\) and so it must be that \(\cl(\span A) \subseteq \cl\span A\text{.}\)
