A family \((e_\alpha)_{\alpha \in A}\) in \(V - \{0\}\) is called an orthogonal system if \(e_\alpha \perp e_\beta\) when \(\alpha \neq \beta\text{.}\) If further, \(\norm{e_\alpha} = 1\) for all \(\alpha \in A\text{,}\) then \((e_\alpha)_\alpha\) is called an orthonormal system.
