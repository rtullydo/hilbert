What we've developed so far gives a hierarchy of structured spaces, each category enveloping the previous; inner product spaces are normed spaces, and normed spaces are metric spaces. We can go one step further and consider more generally linear spaces that have a toplogy but not necessarily a metric. These spaces are called topological vector spaces, which are foundationally important in their own right, but well beyond the scope of this text. If you continue on your journey into Hilbert space theory, you'll meet them in functional analysis.
